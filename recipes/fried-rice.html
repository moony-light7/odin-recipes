Fried Rice

Nigerian fried rice is a flavorful dish made with stir-fried rice, vegetables, and protein, often served at parties and special occasions. It has a distinct taste from curry powder and liver or shrimp.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups parboiled rice
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup cooked liver or shrimp (optional)
  • 1/2 green peas
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook rice in chicken stock until nearly done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions, and add diced liver or shrimp.
  3. Stir in curry powder, thyme, and seasoning cube.
  4. Add carrots, peas, bell peppers, and sweet corn, and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the rice, mix well, and stir-fry for another 5 minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.
