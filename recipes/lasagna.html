Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked to perfection, resulting in a comforting, cheesy delight.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese or bechamel sauce
  • 1 egg(if using ricotta)
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then add tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. If using ricotta, mix it with an egg.
  5. Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
  6. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta (or béchamel), and mozzarella. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbly. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
