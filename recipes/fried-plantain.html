Fried Plantain

Fried plantain, also called "dodo" in Nigeria, is a simple and delicious side dish made from ripe plantains sliced and fried until golden and crispy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Peel plantains and slice into rounds or diagonal pieces
  2. Sprinkle lightly with salt if desired.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry plantain slices until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve as a snack or side dish.
